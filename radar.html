Radar App

A webapp used to share traffic information among the radio stations of Media24 and partners.

  • design
  • coding
  • Firebase

2017

With the expension of our media group the previously used Skype group chat for sharing traffic information became overloaded with data that was not organized and was hard to read. We decided to build a designated web app that would give us control over the data coming in and the way traffic information is displayed to the anchor in real time. At the same time, we wanted to create a uniform sound of the the traffic announcements throghout the media group.

A new solution offered an option to categorize each piece of information

I was the sole UX designer and developer behing the project. I was collaborating with the rest of the radio team on the ideation while being responsible for determining the overall design and developing an app. The app is running using Firebase's Realtime Database

Auto-complete for location input

Previous experience with the existing solution has indicated several problems: some of them being time-consuming data entry, unclear classification/categorization of data and low readability.

Auto-completion option when inputing locations offered a quick solution that ensured speed and accuracy when filling out the form. It also opened the door to future updates and extensions; e.g. we could build a dictionary of locations and their correct pronounciations, which was often an issue on the air.

Editing an existing message

Any additional information can be easily added later on through an option to edit each item.

Individual items can bo reordered using drag & drop based on importance or for location grouping

At the same time, anchors loved the ease of quick access to the needed information which allowed them to read the traffic report straight off of the screen on the fly, without copying each piece of information into a text editor.

This gave them more time to focus on production elements on the show and other content. With an option to re-order items they could easy asure the final text read on the air was consistant and made sense to the listeners.

Traffic Information Center report

A solution also implements a traffic report of the official Traffic Information Center of Slovenia.
